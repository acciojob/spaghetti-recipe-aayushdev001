Sphagetti Recipe


Ingredients

  • Sphagetti
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Salt
  • Water

Instructions

  1. Bring water to boil.
  2. Add sphagetti to boiling water.
    1. Add salt to taste.
    2. Cook for 10 minutes, stirring occassionally.
  3. Add marinara sauce to a pan, bringing to a simmer.
  4. Mix cooked sphagetti with marinanra sauce.
  5. Enjoy.
